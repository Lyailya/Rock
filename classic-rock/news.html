Ultimate Classic Rock is proud to present the exclusive premiere of “Citibank Nile,” a track from Willie Nile’s upcoming World War Willie LP. Due April 1, the album — Nile’s 10th studio effort — follows 2013’s American Ride, and finds him working alongside producer Stewart Lerman and his longtime backing band. The cover shot, which depicts Nile standing in front of a WWII-era landscape of the post-bombing devastation in Dresden, reflects the record’s spirit of resilience in spite of the fact that, as he puts it, “It’s a tough old world out there.”
